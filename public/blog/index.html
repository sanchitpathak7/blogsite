In this blog post I am going over different sorting algorithms as I understand them and also compare their efficieny in terms of time complexity. Insertion Sort Insertion sort breaks the arrays into sub-arrays and sorts them individually, which results in a sorted array. This is a stable sorting algorithm since the relative order will remain the same.
